The Center offers the following services to promote health and wellness for anyone.


Consultations with Doctors


The Center arranges consultation and advice with licensed and self-insured medical doctors of certain specialities. The Center also provides homeopathic consultations by a licensed and self-insured doctor. Except during the pandemic, the Center also organizes monthly mobile health-camp, offering similar services to communities with restricted mobility at host site's facilities. These services are offered on select saturdays of the month and are in-line with the Center's mission of "Love All, Serve All". The Center continues to offer all its services for Humanity - FREE of any returns or remunerations [Note: This is not a medical clinic where Health Insurance is accepted for services].


Yoga & Meditation


Workouts for body, mind and soul that promote overall wellbeing are offered every Saturday at 8am at the Center. Additionally, the Center organizes special workshops by inviting renowned Yoga teachers to offer guided coaching to individuals. These sessions are open for all.


Nutrition Workshops


Eat right and stay healthy! Workshops are conducted at the Center presenting creative culinary ideas that are tasty and zesty! The workshop focuses on a healthy well balanced diet to control common ailments such as blood pressure, diabetes and heart conditions.
