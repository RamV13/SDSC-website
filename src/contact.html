Facilities Rental

If you wish to rent out the facilities for an event, please contact info@saiservice.org.

The building features a 5,600 ft² meeting hall and a 5,600 ft² dining hall with an attached industrial kitchen to warm up food. It is situated in a large, serene wooded landscape with a private lot that can accomodate about 100 cars. The building is equipped with state of the art technology systems such as an audio setup with line array speakers, wired/wireless mics, audio mixing, large LED TV monitors, video distribution, and public Wi-Fi.
