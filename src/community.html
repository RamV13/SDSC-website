The Center performs the following forms of community outreach.


COVID-19 Food Assistance


Center distributes groceries to families in need during the pandemic. Families are identified through a trusted network of partner organizations and volunteers in the community. The families choose the items they need from our flyer which includes fresh produce and dairy. Volunteers at the Center receive calls during the week, procure and pack the customized grocery items ready for a drive-thru contactless pick-up or deliver to their homes if they lack transportation, the following Saturday.

Brunch Bites


Every Saturday, volutneers prepare lunch bags for distribution to the needy in nearby communities.


Howell Food Pantry Supplies Donation

Every month the center organizes a collection drive and donates the non-perishable food supplies to the local food pantries.


Environmental Cleanup


Once a month from the beginning of spring to end of Fall, volunteers clean up the local roads by picking up trash as part of the "clean communities program".


Clothing Drive

In the winter, warm clothing is collected and distributed to the homeless.


Personal Items Drive

Volunteers assemble and distribute hygiene kits to local shelters.


Disaster Relief

Volunteers provide help in various ways such as cash contribution for the disaster relief, cleanup and restoration, conducting special drives and sponsoring reconstruction whenever natural disasters occur anywhere in the world.


Children's Beanies, Blankets, and Sweaters


Volunteers assemble every second Saturday of the month to knit colorful beanies, blankets, and sweaters for for infants of low income and needy families.


Children's Service Day


Once a year, children from various nearby districts assemble to offer a day of service through projects such as making encouraging cards for the sick, knitting blankets, shoe collections, cellphone drives, lens frame drives etc.


Art Classes for Children


Our volunteers at Sarva Dharma Service Center with an artistic talent offer art classes for children under the age of 13 twice a week.
