The mission of Sarva Dharma Service Center, Inc. is to provide humanitarian community services and conduct community activities to promote unity among all by following Bhagavan Sri Sathya Sai Baba's messages of "Love All Serve All" and "Help Ever Hurt Never".

Sathya Sai Baba is one of the most revered spiritual teachers in the world. He is also known as Sri Sathya Sai Baba, Sai Baba, Swami, Bhagavan or simply as Baba to millions of people in the world. You can learn more about Him by clicking here.

Inspired by Baba's teachings and His Love in action, a group of followers from United States went to India in September 1992 and presented to Him a proposal to construct a permanent facility in Mid-Atlantic states of USA which is open for all, to congregate and conduct activities in accordance to His teachings that promote both spiritual and physical well being of an individual. Bhagawan Baba has graciously blessed it and provided guidance over the years. One of His key directives for the Center is to "run it as a service center helping the community and not as a temple". Financial aspects of the project are in strict adherence to the guidelines laid down by Bhagawan. Solicitation of donations or formal fund raising was never done. All funds were and will be from voluntary donations and all donations are kept anonymous except as required by law.

Below is a history of key events in the making of the Center.


1993

November 23

Incorporated a non-profit entity in New Jersey called "Sri Sathya Sai Service Center, Inc." along with the formation of a constitution with by-laws and office bearers

1995

May 8

Entity name changed to "Sarva Dharma Service Center, Inc." to not involve Baba's name in financial and legal contracts

1997

June

Acquired a 9 acre land in Howell Township, New Jersey which is close to major highways

2000

July

Baba reviewed the plans and pronounced "THIS HAS MY GRACE. WITH MY GRACE THIS WILL MATERIALIZE" and suggested a budget

2001

October

The township planning board approved a 16000 Sq. Ft. building on site to be constructed in two phases

2004

August 7

Baba reviewed the plans once again and this time signed on them as a mark of His approval

2006

July

Construction begins with land clearing

2007

November

Phase 1 steel structure completed

2015

March 29

Phase 1 is completed and the building was inaugurated. As per Baba's guidelines the Center immediately began engaging in activities that help the community

April 4

Started free Yoga classes open for all every Saturday 8am at the Center

2016

February 27

Started "Brunch Bites" - 100+ brunch bags with freshly made sandwiches assembled at the Center every Saturday at 9:30am distributed to the needy in nearby communities

May

Started on-site and remote tutoring services teaching math, science and english for under privileged children grade 7-10

2018

August

Started on-site and mobile health and wellness services for people in the local communities. Services include free health consultations, advice on healthy eating and living by certified doctors in Internal Medicine and Homeopathy

...

There are many more community activities kicked off by the Center such as cooking and serving hot meals in soup kitchens, knitting, collecting books, clothing and medical supplies and ship them to under served areas in the world, relief efforts during disasters, and workshops in nutrition and emotional awareness.

Thanks to several anonymous donors and their corporate matching contributions whose generous funding is making a real difference in running this Center and conduct activities that help the community

The volunteers at the Center are in relentless pursuit of new opportunities to serve the community with Love and Humility inspired by Baba's life which is His message to all of humanity to "LOVE ALL, SERVE ALL"
