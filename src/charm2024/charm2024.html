Chandi Sahita Ati Rudra Mahayajna

July 13-21, 2024

With the divine blessings and grace of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba, Chandi Sahita Ati Rudra Mahayajna (CHARM) will be offered by devotees of Bhagawan from all over the world at Sarva Dharma Service Center, Farmingdale, NJ, USA from July 13th to July 21st, 2024. This is the fourth Yajna in a series of five commemorating the Centennial of Advent of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba in 2025 for universal peace.

Please click here for the overview, significance, and details of the offering.

Aligned with Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba's messages of "Love All Serve All" and "Help Ever Hurt Never", Sarva Dharma Service Center (SDSC) has been providing humanitarian community services and conducting community activities to promote unity among all both locally and globally. Specifically, the Center has been providing education & training, health & wellness, basic needs & outreach, disaster relief services for the past 8 years. This Center is uniquely blessed as the only in the United States of America that was personally blessed and built under the guidance of Bhagawan.

There is no fee for participation and the offering is open to all irrespective of caste, creed, religion, color, gender, age, and denomination. You can register to participate as a Ritwik, a Volunteer, or a Devotee. We request each family member to register individually, whether in full or partial attendance.

The entire offering is dedicated for the peace and prosperity of all the beings in all the worlds.

***Ritwik Registration will begin on December 26, 2023 (Datta Purnima) and closes on Feb. 29, 2024***
